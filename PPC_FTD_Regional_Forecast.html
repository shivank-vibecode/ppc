Paid vs Attribution Split

Paid FTDs: FTDs from campaign-days where Spend > 0. These are actively bought with budget and trackable against goal.
Attribution FTDs: FTDs from campaign-days where Spend = 0. These come from the post-click attribution window of paused or aged campaigns. They arrive naturally regardless of current spend decisions and are displayed as a separate baseline — not counted toward the paid goal.
Speed Score: (% Same Day × 0.40) + (% Within 7 Days × 0.30) + (% Within 30 Days × 0.30)
